Disney World Overview

During my visit to Disney World in 2024, I found that most locations were exceptionally accommodating. Many of the restaurants I dined at provided a binder containing detailed information about which foods were safe for various allergies. Below, I will highlight a few noteworthy dining options from the parks.

Hollywood Studios

California Adventure is a park I look forward to visiting for many reasons, the foremost being its Star Wars attractions.

Galaxy`s Edge is the section of the park themed around Star Wars. The standout locations there were Oga`s Cantina and Ronto Roasters. Oga`s isn`t known for its food, but rather for its creatively themed beverages. The server was attentive and provided a binder so we could check which options were available for me. Ronto Roasters was a pleasant surprise, offering a gluten-free pita for their signature wrap.

Epcot

Epcot is a park dedicated to “traveling” the world and celebrating global culture through locations inspired by different countries.

Epcot offered a wide variety of excellent food and experiences. One standout was Tutto Italia. Although the selection was somewhat limited, they were able to prepare most pastas gluten-free, as well as a swordfish steak. Another notable restaurant was Spice Road, located in the Morocco part of the park, which served an incredible spiced lamb kefta.
